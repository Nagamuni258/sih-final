Adventure Trekking

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the majestic Himalayas with our expertly guided trekking expeditions. Our adventure trekking packages are designed for both novice and experienced trekkers, offering breathtaking views, challenging trails, and immersive cultural experiences.

Trekking Highlights

Experience the thrill of reaching high-altitude passes, witness stunning sunrise views over Kanchenjunga, and traverse through lush rhododendron forests and remote mountain villages.

Popular Routes

Kanchenjunga Base Camp, Goecha La Pass, Dzongri Trek, Green Lake Basin

Best Season

March to May and September to November for optimal weather conditions

Difficulty Levels

Easy to challenging options available with professional guides

Accommodation

Luxury campsites, tea houses, and eco-lodges along the routes
