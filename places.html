Himalayan Heritage Resort

Nestled in the heart of the Eastern Himalayas, our luxury resort offers an unparalleled experience of Sikkimese hospitality combined with modern comforts. Enjoy panoramic views of snow-capped peaks while indulging in world-class amenities.

Resort Highlights

The Himalayan Heritage Resort seamlessly blends traditional architecture with contemporary design, offering a tranquil retreat after your day of exploration. Our award-winning spa incorporates ancient Himalayan healing traditions with modern wellness techniques.

Luxury Suites, Heritage Rooms, Mountain View Villas

Amenities

Full-service spa, heated indoor pool, fitness center, yoga studio

Monastery View Lodge

Experience the spiritual heart of Sikkim from our beautifully appointed lodge overlooking the historic Rumtek Monastery. Wake up to the sound of prayer bells and enjoy breathtaking views of both ancient architecture and natural beauty.

Lodge Highlights

Our lodge offers a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the spiritual atmosphere of Sikkim. We organize guided meditation sessions, monastery visits, and cultural evenings where guests can interact with local monks and learn about Buddhist traditions.

Room Types

Monastery View Rooms, Traditional Cottages, Family Suites

Services

Daily meditation sessions, guided monastery tours, traditional tea ceremony

Views

Panoramic views of Rumtek Monastery and Himalayan ranges

Cultural Activities

Prayer flag making, butter sculpture workshops, Buddhist philosophy discussions

Starting from ₹5,200 per night

Tea Garden Homestay

Experience authentic Sikkimese hospitality at our family-run homestay surrounded by lush organic gardens. Our homestay offers a unique opportunity to live with a local family, participate in daily activities, and enjoy home-cooked traditional meals.

Homestay Highlights

Our homestay is more than just accommodation - it's an immersive cultural experience. Guests can participate in gardening, cooking classes, and traditional craft workshops. We source most of our ingredients from our organic garden, ensuring fresh and healthy meals.

Organic Garden

Guests can participate in harvesting and cooking with fresh produce

Accommodation

Private rooms with attached bathrooms in a traditional Sikkimese house

Meals

Home-cooked traditional meals with the family using organic ingredients

Experiences

Cooking classes, traditional craft workshops, local guided walks

Starting from ₹2,800 per night (including meals)
